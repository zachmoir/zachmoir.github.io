Meet our amazing team.

The MC2 team works hard to bring innovative solutions to your business.

Caroline Copeman, BSc, MBA

Caroline has over two decades of strategic and operational leadership as a business advisor, management consultant, entrepreneur, business owner, corporate executive and board director. Caroline’s business passion is innovation and entrepreneurship. She has worked extensively in the food and beverage, life sciences, technology and professional services sectors. Areas of expertise include corporate strategy, business planning, business expansion, sales and business development, and market entry strategy. As an entrepreneur and business owner, Caroline has hands-on experience with business challenges, including strategy development, shareholder management, debt, equity and government grant financing, M&A strategy and execution, and market development, Active in her community in a variety of roles focusing on innovation, Caroline is currently a mentor for business owners in the food and beverage sector. Caroline is an experienced executive and board director and can provide interim board and management services for businesses.

Caroline Copeman
Managing Director

Michele Middlemore, CPA, CA, CFA, CBV

Michele has over 20 years of M&A and corporate finance experience in a variety of industries. She works primarily with mid-market businesses providing advice and hands on assistance with growth strategies, valuation, raising debt and equity capital, acquisitions, divestitures and succession/transition planning. In addition to her extensive advisory experience, Michele also headed the acquisitions department of a large Canadian corporation where she was responsible for the identification and execution of strategic acquisitions nationally as well as the planning and management of post-merger integration activities.

Michele Middlemore
Managing Director

Cindy Wilson, CPA, CA, CFA

Cindy has 20 years of experience in M&A and corporate finance advisory across various industries, including technology, healthcare, food and beverage, hospitality and consumer products. She has worked with businesses of all sizes to provide advice and hands on assistance with growth strategies, acquisitions, divestitures, capital raising (debt and equity), valuations and succession/transition planning. An entrepreneur herself, Cindy has a particular appreciation for the challenges and opportunities facing business owners. She combines her depth of understanding, breadth of relationships with operators, lenders and investors, together with her diverse experience to help business owners develop creative solutions to achieve their goals.
