About MC2

MC2 Business Advisors Inc. is a boutique advisory firm providing a full range of strategy, operations, M&A and corporate finance advisory services for business owners and small to mid-size enterprises at all stages of the business life cycle, including start-up operations.

Owned by three women with more than 60 years of combined experience, we will help you take your business to the next level. Our expertise extends across a broad range of industries including healthcare, consumer products, manufacturing, technology, food & beverage, professional services, hospitality and retail sectors.

We believe in your great ideas, see what we can do with you

As business owners ourselves, we know how day to day decisions can take up your time. We know that it can be difficult to find moments to plan strategically and to consider the big picture for you and the business. And even more difficult to bring your big ideas to life.

We are here to help you. Whether you want to expand into new markets organically, buy a business to extend your reach, or sell your business to make space for some other dream to take root, we can assist.
