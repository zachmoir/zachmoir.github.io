Welcome To Symcor Case Competition

Open to all 3rd, 4th, and 5th year students, Symcor Case Competition, hosted by the Information Technology Management Students’ Association, in collaboration with McMaster University, University of Toronto, and York University, aims to bridge the gap between the theoretical aspects of technology learned at school with the practical aspects from the IT field. On January 27th, 2018, delegates will compete head-to-head to challenge their capabilities, defy boundaries, and put their skills to the test.

Ted Rogers School of Management

Management Technology Association

Recognizing the need for Management and IT skill sets in today's world, our goal is to help develop students in need to achieve their development goals. With the Management and IT stream being the longest and the most rigorous program academically, we started this club to create a community among students passionate in Management and IT in order for them to get the support they need from peers, upper years, and professionals. By providing various opportunities, events, and being a center of information, we create growth opportunities students need to strive academically and in the workplace.

McMaster Engineering and Management Society

The McMaster Engineering and Management Society (MEMS) is a group who works to develop an established leadership community that provides engineering and management students with the resources to develop professionally, socially and personally. MEMS creates a strong sense of community based around program identity for the Engineering and Management program, by setting up networking events, job fairs, and information sessions curated towards students in this stream.

Information Technology Students Association

The Information Technology Student Association (ITSA) is a non-profit student-run organization that caters to York University students in the Information Technology, Business, and Computer Science disciplines. We aim to create academic, social, and professional-related experiences for our student body through various events during the year.

ITMSA

ITMSA is the the premier course union and student association for the Business Technology Management program at Ted Rogers School of Management at Ryerson University.
