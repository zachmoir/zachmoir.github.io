Welcome To Symcor Case Competition

Symcor case competition is a partnership of students, sponsors, proffesors, and alumni working together to assist students in acquiring the skills needed for the future. Throughout these two days, we hope to provide you a competitive environment to leverage information technology, while solving challenging business problems to succeed within the industry.

group

Network

Expand your knowledge while understanding different perspectives and disscusing feedback on Friday’s networking session or Saturday’s social. Learn and raise your profile while increasing your connections!

trending_up

Growth

Gain insight on the non-stop progressing IT industry and emerge yourself further into a world filled with crucial, agile technology. Seize and take advantage of the opportunities, all while continuing to grow.

check

Skills

Bridge the gap between theoretical aspects learnt at school and the practical aspects of the IT feild. By leveraging technology to solve business problems, you will further improve upon soft skills such as, critcal thinking, communication, etc.

Develop connections

Along with competing against teams from ADD UNIS, delegates will have opportunities throughout Friday and Saturday to gain insight from various speakers such as industry proffesionals, proffesors, and alumni. Delegates will also be provided with a networking session to strengthen connections while showcasing and improving upon pre-existing skills.

Relax and unwind

Subsequently, after two days of intense competition, we have arranged a social held at NAME to unwind and celebrate our delegates and partners. With your support, Symcor case compeition remains a valuable annual event hosted by the ITMSA!
