Strategy

Business planning

Every business needs a good plan that is revisited and updated annually. MC2 will support all your planning needs, from setting out a longer term strategic plan to annual operational plans. MC2 will provide all the financial forecasting and modelling, market research, competitive assessment and customer surveys needed to ensure your plans are informed, relevant, actionable, and useful for your team.

Business expansion advisory

MC2 can help you determine where and how to expand your business with market research and market entry strategy development. MC2 will conduct market research into such areas as size, geography, demographics, competition and consumer profiles and support that with primary market research through focus groups, interviews and surveys. MC2 will then compile and analyse the information and work with you to develop your market entry strategy. This could include product description, pricing, distribution channels, promotion and proposed business model. MC2 will develop a financial model and forecasts to support the market entry strategy.
